Pasta Recipe Below:

Description

Have you ever tried pasta that brings out every flavour? Where you can taste, smell and feel every step taken to create this masterpiece? If not, you've come to the right place! This pasta is simply an art piece but instead of being pleasant in the eyes, it is pleasant for your mouth and stomach!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Mint

Steps

  1. Start by filling a small pot with water more than halfway.
  2. Once it starts boiling, pour your pasta in.
  3. Wait 10 minutes and check if the pasta is soft every 2 minutes.
  4. Once the pasta is done,drain the water using a drainer.
  5. After pouring the water out, put it back on the stove on low heat and pour your tomato sauce and cheese in.
  6. As you mix it, everything should come together and the pasta is done.
  7. Serve it in a bowl and sprinkle some mint leaves on.

Serve and Enjoy!
