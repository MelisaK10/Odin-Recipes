Haleem Recipe Below:

Description

Hello! I will be sharing a juicy and mouth-watering recipe. The taste of the finished haleem will take you back to your homeland, where homemade dishes were always preferred over fast-food. Where just the aroma of the dish made your mouth water. Stay tuned for this ecstatic and exciting step by step recipe.

Ingredients

  • Barley
  • Wheat
  • Rice
  • Yellow Lentil
  • Red Lentil
  • Salt
  • Green Chili
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Beef
  • Haleem Spice Mix
  • Red Chili
  • Cumin Seeds

Steps

  1. Cook grains and lentils until soft and mushy. Then blend it with an immersion blender.
  2. Cook meat and shreds it in a processor.
  3. Mix wheat and meat curry and finish it with tempering.
  4. Wash the wheat and Barley until the water runs clear. Soak both for minimum 4 hours
  5. Discard water after wait time, Transfer both to a pot.
  6. Add Ginger, Garlic and Chili paste. Fillpot with water(a bit more than halfway).
  7. Bring water to boil, reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and cook for 60-90 minutes.
  8. Fry onions in seperate pot.
  9. Once golden/brown, add meat and spices.
  10. Once the colour of the meat beomes light brown,add water and mix occasionally.
  11. Remove meat from the curry with slotted spoon.
  12. Add gravy to grains and lentil ad blend it with an immersion blender.
  13. Seperate meat from bones, shred meat in food processor. Add shredded meat to haleem.
  14. Add a bit of yogurt. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  15. Garnish with ginger, coriander and green chili!

Enjoy!
